Marry Me Chicken

Home

Description

Marry Me Chicken is creamy, juicy, and full of flavor! It's said that this chicken dinner is so good that if you cook it for your partner, they will propose to you! Yes, it's THAT amazing! Funny name, but probably the best chicken recipe ever!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced into cutlets
  • 50g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • dried oregano
  • Paprika
  • dried thyme
  • dried basil
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • chicken stock
  • thickened cream
  • 250g penne
  • grated parmesan

Method

  1. Add flour, pepper, salt, paprika, garlic powder and onion powder into a bowl, whisking to combine
  2. Coat chicken cutlets on both sides with teh flour mixture and set aside
  3. Heat up a large saucepan on medium and add butter and olive oil, allowing to heat for 1 minute before adding the chicken. Allow to cook fully on both sides and get a nice golden crust. Remove from the pan and set aside
  4. Sautee garlic and chili flakes for 30 seconds before adding chicken stock and cream. Stir to coming then add oregano and thyime, dried basil and onion powder
  5. Bring to a simmer and cook for 12-15 minutes, adding chicken back in in the last 5 minutes
  6. Cook pasta in heavily seasoned water until al dente. Remove the chicken from the pan and toss through the pasta in the sauce
  7. Toss through parmesan cheese and choped parsley and basil
  8. Add chicken back in and serve
